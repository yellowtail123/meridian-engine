Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Meridian Engine is a client-side research tool. Here is how your data is handled:

Data Storage

All your research data — saved papers, collections, chat history, screening decisions, and settings — is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB and localStorage. Nothing is sent to or stored on our servers.

Third-Party APIs

When you use Meridian's features, your search queries and coordinates are sent to these public academic APIs:

  • Literature: OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, CrossRef, PubMed (NCBI)
  • Species: WoRMS, GBIF, OBIS, FishBase, BOLD Systems
  • Environmental: NOAA ERDDAP, Open-Meteo, NOAA Mauna Loa
  • Maps: OpenStreetMap tiles, Nominatim geocoding

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. Meridian sends only the minimum data required (search terms, coordinates, DOIs).

AI API Keys

If you use the AI Research Assistant, you must provide your own Anthropic API key. Your key is stored in memory only — it is never saved to disk, never sent to our servers, and is automatically cleared when you close the tab. All API calls go directly from your browser to Anthropic.

Analytics

We use privacy-respecting, cookie-free analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand aggregate usage. No personal data is collected, and no tracking cookies are set.
